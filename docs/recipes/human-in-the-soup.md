## Background

Every burgeoning poet at any stage in their work is looking for something
sustainable in the latest literary stream. The "Cybertext" of the late 1990s
seems today increasingly capable of operating without human intervention. The
discourse plate introduced here suggests that generative systems might be a
working answer: not by pushing out the human, but by making collaboration the
form itself.

## Ingredients

- WiFi
- A laptop
- The free app [PoemChef](https://poemchef.andrewklobucar.com/)

**Preparation time:** 5 minutes per course for a 30-minute discourse plate

## The Discourse Plate: Six Courses

Opening the app provides an easy way to produce different genres of poetry.
Clicking any genre opens your workspace ‚Äî the poetry kitchen. For each course,
choose your ingredients (themes) and at least two literary devices, then
generate and complete lines as the poem chef.

## Notes

The aim of becoming a skilled Poem Chef is to maintain control over your
creations, as future generative systems will continue to challenge us to keep
the human element somewhere in the process. While interacting with the software,
the poem chef maintains the choice of completing any number of lines while
allowing the tool to generate more if necessary. The finished work remains
cybernetic ‚Äî a new generative system as writing partner.
